Introduction to Cooking

A hands-on course that introduces learners to essential cooking skills, food preparation, and basic nutrition principles for everyday meals.

Syllabus

Week 1: Kitchen Safety & Hygiene
Week 2: Cooking Tools & Techniques
Week 3: Meal Planning and Basic Recipes
Week 4: Nutrition & Healthy Cooking
Week 5: Local Dishes and Creative Presentation
Week 6: Final Assessment and Group Cook-Off

Outcomes

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:
- Understand and apply kitchen safety and hygiene practices.
- Use basic cooking tools and techniques effectively.
- Plan and prepare simple, nutritious meals.
- Explore local dishes and enhance food presentation skills.
