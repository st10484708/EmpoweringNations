Gardening

🌿

Purpose

To equip individuals with the practical knowledge and skills needed to grow, maintain, and care for various plants, flowers, and vegetables, promoting sustainable gardening and self-sufficiency.

📘

What you'll Learn

  • Soil preparation and composting techniques
  • Planting and watering methods for different plant types
  • Pest and disease control using natural methods
  • Seasonal planting and garden maintenance
  • Container gardening and landscape design basics
🕒

Time frame

6-months to complete
